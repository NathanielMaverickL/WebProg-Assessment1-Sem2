Characters

Lorelai Gilmore
A witty and independent single mother raising her daughter Rory in Stars Hollow.

Rory Gilmore
Lorelai’s intelligent and ambitious daughter, striving for academic success.

Luke Danes
The grumpy yet kind-hearted diner owner who has a soft spot for Lorelai.

Emily Gilmore
Lorelai’s wealthy and opinionated mother who desires a close relationship with Rory.

Richard Gilmore
Lorelai’s intelligent and business-savvy father who values tradition.

Sookie St. James
Lorelai’s best friend and an enthusiastic, talented chef.

Paris Geller
Rory’s competitive and determined classmate who becomes her friend.

Jess Mariano
Luke’s rebellious nephew who forms a strong connection with Rory.

Dean Forester
Rory’s first boyfriend, known for his kindness and loyalty.

Logan Huntzberger
A charming and wealthy Yale student who becomes Rory’s boyfriend.

Kirk Gleason
A quirky and eccentric Stars Hollow resident with many odd jobs.

Lane Kim
Rory’s music-loving best friend who struggles with her strict upbringing.
