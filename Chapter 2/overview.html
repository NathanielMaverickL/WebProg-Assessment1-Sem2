Overview

Gilmore Girls is an American TV series (2000â€“2007) created by Amy Sherman-Palladino. It follows the lives of Lorelai Gilmore, a witty single mother, and her intelligent daughter, Rory, in the charming town of Stars Hollow. The show explores their close mother-daughter bond, love lives, career ambitions, and relationships with the wealthy Gilmore family. Known for its fast-paced dialogue, pop culture references, and heartwarming yet dramatic storytelling, the series remains a beloved classic. A revival, Gilmore Girls: A Year in the Life, was released on Netflix in 2016.
